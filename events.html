Full Moon Wellness Retreat

A unique 3-day wellness retreat designed around the full moon's energy, combining spiritual and physical healing. Guests can engage in activities such as moonlight yoga, guided meditation sessions, and aromatherapy workshops. The event includes a special lunar-themed spa treatment, such as a moonstone massage and energy-cleansing rituals. On the final night, a moonlit dinner by the beach will feature organic dishes crafted to restore balance and vitality. Guests will leave feeling refreshed, rejuvenated, and in tune with their mind, body, and spirit.

International Wine & Dine Experience

Oasis Spa and Resort will host an exclusive 4-day wine and dining experience that brings together expert sommeliers and top chefs from around the world. Each day will highlight the culinary traditions of a different country, from French Bordeaux wines paired with classic French cuisine to Italian wines enjoyed with the finest pasta dishes. Special guests include winemakers and chefs offering insights into pairing, preparation, and the cultural significance of each dish and wine. In addition to meals, there will be wine-tasting masterclasses and a guided vineyard tour.
